About the Event

The Tourism Cares Event is an inclusivity initiative that promotes awareness and celebrates the fact that people with disabilities can and should be a part of tourism. It is proudly organized by second-year students pursuing a Diploma in Tourism and Event Management at the Central University of Technology, Free State.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build an inclusive tourism community where everyone, regardless of ability, can fully participate and create lasting memories.

Mission: Our mission is to raise awareness, break barriers, and advocate for accessibility in tourism. We aim to show that tourism is for everyone and that inclusivity strengthens our industry and communities.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness about inclusivity in tourism.
  • Encourage accessible tourism opportunities for people with disabilities.
  • Promote community unity and collaboration in the tourism sector.
  • Educate stakeholders about the importance of accessibility in tourism facilities and services.
  • Inspire lasting change towards equality and inclusivity.
